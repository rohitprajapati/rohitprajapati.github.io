What is Home Tandoor ?

A home tandoor is a type of clay oven that is commonly used in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisine. It is typically made of clay or ceramic and is heated by burning wood or charcoal inside the oven. The high heat of the tandoor allows food to cook quickly and evenly, while also giving it a distinct smoky flavor.


Home tandoors are often used to cook a variety of dishes, including breads, meats, and vegetables. They can be used indoors or outdoors, and come in a range of sizes to suit different needs. Some home tandoors are portable and can be used on a tabletop, while others are larger and require a dedicated space in the kitchen or outdoor area.


To use a home tandoor, you will need to preheat it by lighting a fire inside the oven. Once the tandoor is hot, you can place the food on skewers or in a special tandoori pot and cook it inside the oven. Tandoors are known for producing delicious and flavorful food, and are a popular choice for those who enjoy traditional or authentic Indian and Middle Eastern cuisine.
